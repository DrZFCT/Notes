\section{Tensor Algebra}

Let $\Gamma^{r,s}(M)$ denote the space of $(r,s)$ tensors on $M$.

Given a coordinate $A\in\Gamma^{r,s}$ can be written as
\begin{equation}
    A=A^{i_1,\dots,i_r}_{j_1,\dots,j_s}\partial_{i_1}\otimes\cdots\otimes\partial_{i_r}\otimes dx_{j_1}\otimes\cdots\otimes dx_{j_s},
\end{equation}

tangent vectors $\Gamma(M)=\Gamma^{1,0}(M)$

one-forms $\Gamma^*(M)=\Gamma^{0,1}(M)$
